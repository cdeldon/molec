void molec_set_initial_condition ( molec_Simulation_SOA_t *  simulation)

Initialize the simulation (position, velocity and force) by applying initial condition.

We will initially position the particles on a regular grid and randomly offset them later. To construct the regular grid, we place particles along each axis with a spread of between the individual particles where represent the extend of the simulation.

The particles are offset by choosing a uniform random number in . To make sure the particles won't be placed to close to each other we scale the resulting offset with molec_parameter->scaling

Note: the simulation struct must be fully allocated (including the individual arrays).
